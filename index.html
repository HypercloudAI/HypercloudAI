About

Hypercloud Intelligence is an engineering initiative founded and led by Ariyan Ahmed (online: BloxyWave). The project focuses on compact models, optimization pipelines, and production-grade packaging to enable local, low-cost AI deployments.

Engineering priorities include architecture research, quantization and pruning toolchains, runtime optimization for target platforms, and rigorous benchmarking to ensure reproducibility.

Declaration
We are working to build an AI model without assistance from OpenAI, Gemini, or any external LLM. Our objective is to create powerful AI that is free to use and deployable locally.
Platform focus update
During early development we focused on Android. Moving forward, Hypercloud Intelligence is prioritizing Windows-only projects. This strategic shift enables deeper native integration, expanded tooling, and access to larger development compute resources.

Technical goals

  • Maximize compute efficiency per parameter.
  • Enable Windows-first inference with low power draw and developer tooling.
  • Open toolchains for quantization, deployment, and reproducibility.
  • Rigorous benchmarking, safety audits, and reproducible experiments.

Ethics & Safety

Releases follow safety-first practices including adversarial testing, access controls, and transparency. Community contributions are reviewed for safety, reproducibility, and license compatibility.
